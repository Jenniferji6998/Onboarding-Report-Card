Report Card Details

How the evaluations are created:

All report card scores and assessments are generated using transcript data from Revenue Onboarding live sessions, analyzed by AI against role-specific and core competency metrics. This ensures that each evaluation reflects actual participation and engagement using an objective, data-driven process.

Note: Seismic Completion Rate and Badge Overview Chart are specifically based on each new hire's progress as of their week 5 mark in the program.

Why this is valuable for you:

  • Clear Progress Tracking: Visualize your new hire's journey across all onboarding touchpoints.
  • Benchmarking: Easily compare your new hire's progress to peers in their cohort.
  • Actionable Insights & Coaching: Quickly spot where your support will have the most impact.
  • At-a-Glance Data: Save time and make informed, proactive decisions with concise visual summaries.
